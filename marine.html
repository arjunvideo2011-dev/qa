About Marine Life

Marine life includes all plants and animals living in oceans and seas. It includes fish, coral reefs, whales, dolphins, and many tiny organisms. Oceans cover most of the Earth and support life on our planet.


Why Marine Life is Important

Oceans provide food, oxygen, and help regulate Earth’s climate. Healthy marine ecosystems support biodiversity and human life. Protecting marine life keeps the planet balanced.


How to Protect Marine Life

Reduce plastic use, avoid ocean pollution, and protect coral reefs. Save water and support clean oceans. Small actions can save marine life for the future.
